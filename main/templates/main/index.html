Welcome to Our Department

Department of Computer Science, Mankar College, has been imparting quality education to students for over two decades. This department is a pioneer in starting undergraduate Computer Science courses under the University of Burdwan, way back in 1999. Department has already undertaken many research projects funded by various funding agencies.

The department has modern well-designed classrooms and is well equipped for adopting modern teaching aids like LCD projectors, Smart Class Rooms, etc. The laboratories are well equipped with the latest equipment, demonstration kits, charts, etc. Effective and reasonable use of ICT - Google form of assignments is encouraged among students.
